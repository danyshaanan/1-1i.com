What is this about?

This is a collection of mathematical graphics generated by Python scripts.

The point is;
    to plainly explain the meaning of the mathematical objects,
    to present them in an aesthetically pleasing way,
    and to let visitors see, download, and play with the scripts that generate them.

Feel free to contact me through the mail address in my home page.
